# Refund Policy

## Overview

This document outlines the refund policies for all customers using Acme Cloud services.

## Standard Plan

Customers on the Standard Plan are eligible for a refund within 14 days of purchase if the service has not been heavily used.

Conditions:

- Refund requests must be submitted through the billing portal.
- Refunds are processed within 7 business days.

## Enterprise Plan

Enterprise customers are eligible for refunds within 30 days of purchase.

Additional conditions:

- Refund requests must be approved by the account manager.
- Partial refunds may be issued depending on service usage.

## Non-refundable Items

The following purchases are non-refundable:

- Custom integrations
- Professional services
- Training packages
